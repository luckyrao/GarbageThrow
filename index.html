How to Play

  1. Pick a garbage object. Different shapes have different drag.
  2. Adjust angle and power to match the moving bin.
  3. Watch wind and gusts (fans/windows) change during the throw.
  4. Land the garbage in the bin to advance to the next level.

Physics focus: projectile motion, vectors, drag, mass, acceleration, and relative motion.

Built as a fun physics playground for all ages.
